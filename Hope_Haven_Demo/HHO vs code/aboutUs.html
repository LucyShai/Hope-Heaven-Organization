  • OUR AIM

    Our aim is to work as a middleman to supply essential
    item such as food,clothes,blankets,minor furnitures,
    etc.,to less privileged people. Our donors will first register
    and then log in.The donors select items they are willing to
    donate and specify if they are willing to deliver them or
    provide a location for us to pick them up. If it is money,
    banking details will be provided. Donors can individually
    track the items they have donated,as we are focusing not
    only in organisations but also on community members.
    For reassurance, our donors will be notified when their
    items will be delivered so they can witness the entire
    delivery process.

  •  

  • OUR INSPIRATION

    Our inspiration came from realizing that people think
    donating takes a lot,they think it only takes millions to
    change lives.However, changing people's situations can
    take just a rand and some second-hand clothes. The
    majority of us grew up around young and old people who
    find themselves in disadvantaged situations where they
    can't provide for themselves and don't have the power to
    change their circumstances. Hope Haven Organization
    can be helping hand.
